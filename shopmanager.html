The Stock Room

Once you have selected a shop you can add items to your shop from the Stock Room!










Add New Item To Stock Room

Any item you add here will be added to the Stock Room. To add an item to your shop a shop must be selected, then you can choose an item from the Stock Room.



Create and Manage Your Shops

Enter a unique campaign id. Your shops will be saved to this id, so don't forget it! You will give your players this id so that they can access the shops, as well.

Once you've entered your campaign id, select a shop from the dropdown menu or create a new one with the New Shop button. Once a shop has been selected, you can start adding items from the Stock Room at the top of the page!
